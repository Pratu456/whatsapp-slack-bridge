Common questions
How long does activation take?
Most accounts are activated within 24 hours of registering. You'll receive an email with your claim code once activated.
What is a claim code?
A claim code is a unique word your customers send as their first WhatsApp message. It links them to your Slack workspace. You receive it in your activation email.
Can I change my claim code?
Yes. Contact us and we can regenerate your claim code. Note that existing contacts will need to send the new code to re-link.
How do I delete my account?
Email us at support@syncora.app with your company name and we'll delete your account and all associated data within 48 hours.
