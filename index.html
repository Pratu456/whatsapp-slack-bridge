Security & privacy
Your data is safe with us

We built Syncora with privacy first. Here's exactly how we protect your business and your customers.

Every company on Syncora operates in a completely isolated environment. Your messages, contacts, and workspace are never visible to — or accessible by — any other company.

Customers connect voluntarily by sending your claim code — explicit, informed consent. You stay in control of your data at all times.

🔒
Is my data safe?
▼
Yes. Every company has completely isolated data. No company can ever see another company's messages, contacts, or conversations. Your data belongs to you only.
👁️
Who can see our conversations?
▼
Only your team in your own Slack workspace. Syncora routes messages but does not read or store conversation content beyond what's needed to deliver it. Nobody at Syncora reads your chats.
📞
What about customer phone numbers?
▼
Phone numbers are stored securely and linked only to your workspace. They are never shared with other companies or sold to third parties.
🇪🇺
Is this GDPR compliant?
▼
Yes. Customers voluntarily connect by sending your claim code — this is explicit, informed consent. You can remove any contact at any time. All data can be permanently deleted on request.
🚪
What if we want to stop?
▼
You can deactivate your workspace at any time from the admin panel. All your company data can be permanently deleted on request — no questions asked, no lock-in.
⚠️
What are the risks?
▼
Syncora uses Twilio's WhatsApp API and Slack's official OAuth — enterprise-grade infrastructure. The main thing to manage: keep your claim code private. If shared publicly, unintended contacts could reach your workspace. You can regenerate it anytime from the admin panel.
